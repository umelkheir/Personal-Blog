Mouton was born in 1929, in Fairfax, Virginia to Rhodie and Edna Chloe.
She graduated from Howard University in 1950 with a master's degree in mathematics, after receiving a bachelor's degree in mathematics with a minor in physics.
She started working for NASA in 1959, after working for the Army Map Service and the Census Bureau.
The following year, Echo 1 was put into orbit, and Mouton lead a team of NASA mathematicians (known as "computers") in tracking its orbit
